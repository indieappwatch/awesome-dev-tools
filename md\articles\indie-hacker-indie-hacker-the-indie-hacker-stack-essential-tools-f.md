# The Indie Hacker Stack: Essential Tools for Bootstrapped Founders in 2024

> A curated guide covering the best landing-page tools, platforms, and workflows for modern developers and indie hackers.

Building a business without venture capital forces you to be intentional about every tool you adopt. The indie hacker tool decisions aren't just about features — they're about runway, complexity, and whether a tool will still be around in two years. Here's what stacks up.

## Featured Tools & Platforms


### [Carrd](https://carrd.co)

A dead-simple site builder for one-page sites. Perfect for landing pages, product links, and pre-launch captures. Costs almost nothing and takes minutes to set up.

**Highlight**: Build beautiful one-page sites in minutes


### [Gumroad](https://gumroad.com)

Sell digital products, memberships, and one-time purchases with almost zero friction. The overlay checkout and link-based selling make it uniquely founder-friendly.

**Highlight**: The easiest way to sell digital products directly


### [Nomad List](https://nomadlist.com)

A community-curated database of cities ranked by cost, internet speed, weather, and quality of life. Indispensable for digital nomads choosing their next base.

**Highlight**: Find your next city with community-driven data


### [Headline](https://headline.co)

AI-powered headline analyzer trained on viral content. Enter your headline and get instant feedback on emotional impact, power words, and readability.

**Highlight**: AI headlines that actually drive clicks


### [Notion](https://www.notion.so)

The workspace that replaced a dozen different tools for indie founders. Documentation, project management, wikis, and databases — all in one flexible system.

**Highlight**: The all-in-one workspace for indie teams


### [Linear](https://linear.app)

Issue tracking built for modern software teams. The keyboard-first interface and beautiful design make project management feel less like overhead.

**Highlight**: Issue tracking that developers actually enjoy using


### [Beamer](https://www.beamer.com)

A changelog and roadmap tool that keeps users informed without clogging their inbox. Embeddable newsfeed widget for your product.

**Highlight**: Keep users engaged with a beautiful changelog


### [Typefully](https://typefully.app)

A minimalist writing environment for Twitter threads and LinkedIn posts. AI suggestions, scheduled publishing, and analytics — all distraction-free.

**Highlight**: Write and schedule Twitter threads without friction


### [Lemonsqueezy](https://www.lemonsqueezy.com)

Merchant of record that handles taxes, invoicing, and global compliance for digital products. Perfect for indie hackers selling internationally without a legal team.

**Highlight**: Global payments without the compliance headache


### [OpenPhone](https://openphone.com)

A modern business phone service built for startups. Voicemail transcription, call recording, and team numbers — all in a clean app.

**Highlight**: Business phone that fits modern startup workflows
