About Cursor

Cursor represents a new category of development tool — one where AI isn't an add-on but the foundation of the entire experience. Built on VS Code's codebase, it brings the familiar interface while integrating AI assistance throughout the editing workflow in ways that genuinely speed up development.

The AI chat panel understands your codebase context, can read files, and help debug issues or explain unfamiliar code. The Composer feature lets you orchestrate multiple files at once, making it feel less like you're writing code and more like you're directing a knowledgeable assistant. For repetitive boilerplate, the AI can generate and edit code with your specifications.

What makes Cursor genuinely different from adding Copilot to VS Code is how deeply the AI is integrated into the editing experience. Inline edits understand your codebase, the diff view shows exactly what changed, and the AI remembers your preferences and coding style. For solo developers and small teams, this level of assistance can compress timelines significantly.

Key Features
