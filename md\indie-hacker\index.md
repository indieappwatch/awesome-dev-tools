# Indie Hacker Resources

The indie hacker movement has created a new path to building software businesses. By focusing on profitable growth rather than venture funding, solo founders and small teams are shipping products that serve real niches.

The tools in this space reflect the pragmatic choices of builders who count every dollar. Free tiers, usage-based pricing, and minimal dependencies mean you can start without risk and scale when you have revenue.

From accepting your first payment to managing customer relationships, these resources form the toolkit of the modern indie business.

## Popular Tools in This Category

- **Stripe** — Payment infrastructure for internet businesses
- **Notion** — The connected workspace for your wiki, docs, and projects
- **Zapier** — Connect your apps and automate workflows
- **Supabase** — The open source Firebase alternative with a Postgres foundation
- **Railway** — Infrastructure made simple for developers
